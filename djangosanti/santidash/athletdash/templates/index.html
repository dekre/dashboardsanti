DHTMLX Sidebar Demo

This is an interactive demonstration of dhtmlxSidebar functionality. The demo represents a JavaScript sidebar on the left side of the page together with dhtmlxToolbar (on top) and dhtmlxLayout. The sidebar contains:

  • navigation items equipped with icons
  • separators
  • spacers
  • blue and red notification badges with numbers

The toggle button allows you to collapse the sidebar.
